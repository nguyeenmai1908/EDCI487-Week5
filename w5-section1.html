Welcome & Framing
~3 minutes

We have spent the first four weeks close to the ground — with stories, bodies, neighbourhoods, and relationships. With Krenak's river. With the trees missing from certain streets. With the false promises made in the name of green.

Now we zoom out. Not to abandon what we found there, but to ask a different question: why is the system that produces these harms so difficult to change?

The answer has everything to do with the kind of problem climate change actually is. It is not a simple problem — one cause, one fix. It is a complex adaptive system: interconnected, dynamic, full of feedback loops and emergent surprises. Understanding this changes everything about how we teach it.
